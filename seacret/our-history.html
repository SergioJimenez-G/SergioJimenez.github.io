OUR HISTORY
How we started our Seacret journey

When I first started my direct selling business a month and half ago, my wife was working full-time and I was searching for a career change which would be flexible enough that I could continue to stay at home with my two children. I wanted to make my own hours and make enough money to provide for my family.

Luckily, my sister-in-law reached out and introduced us to Seacret Direct. I was amazed by the business structure, compensation plan, products, and most of all the fantastic support within the company. This company promotes and teaches leadership to foster all agents to achieve their highest potential. It is by far the most outstanding company in the relationship marketing world.

The response to the products has been bar none! These products are helping people with not only cosmetic issues, but chronic skin conditions! Within five months, we built our business to over 280 agents and customers. My goal with Seacret is to establish a strong income with financial freedom for my family, and help my team members achieve their personal goals as well.

My beautiful wife and myself in Santander, Spain.
