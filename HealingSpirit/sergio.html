About Me

My name is Sergio Jimenez and I am originally from Madrid, Spain. I am a stay at home dad, have a web development company, and I am also a full-time student pursuing another Bachelor’s Degree in Web Development. I enjoy spending time with my wife, running, biking, and living a healthy life style. However, I spend most of my time taking care of my two greatest accomplishments, my children. I recently earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Cartography with a Minor in Computer Science. Prior to going to school, I worked several years in the computer industry. Computer science is a big part of my life, but music is my greatest passion. If you want to check out my Dj sets, please go to my personal website at Sergiojimenez.com.

When I was younger, I used to believe that wellness was solely based on physical activity. Over the years, I have realized that overall personal wellness incorporates continual growth in mind, body, and spirit. I am always trying to better myself and constantly studying information related to spiritual and physical development. Currently, I have found the combination of using essential oils, meditating, and practicing yoga to have a profound impact on my well-being. I would love to share this information with others and also receive input from other people about their experiences with wellness practices
