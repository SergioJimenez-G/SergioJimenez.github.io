Hi, it's me!

My name is Sergio Jimenez and I am originally from Madrid, Spain. Music is my greatest passion. I have over 20 years of experience and specialize in techno house, progressive, trance and electronic styles.

While in Spain, I had a successful career as a DJ for large clubs in the Madrid and Ibiza areas providing both indoor and outdoor performances.
