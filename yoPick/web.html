Web Development Services

Currently, it is essential that a company has presence on the Internet. At yopick, we specialize in developing websites and advanced web portals tailored to the client. Whatever the size of your project or your organization, we will offer several alternatives for the development and design of your company website, adapting perfectly to the needs of your business. Our company is focused on developing user-friendly websites that will allow the customer complete autonomy for self-management of the website.

An E-commerce website can reach many more potential customers and keep your store open 24 hours a day, breaking the geographical and time barriers. An online store will provide reduced costs, increased service quality, and generate a much more interactive relationship with customers. With our Online shops, you will quickly increase your sales.

While we know that design is always subjective, there are very clear definitions as to the quality of development that must not fail to be taken into account when doing a professional job. We adhere to Web solution standards which are important when creating a web develpment project:

  • Ensure that the website is well designed and with a proper assembly and architecture.
  • Ensure that your website works always and in all Internet browsers.
  • The content of your site will be correctly interpreted by search engines.

The development of compatible website standards ensures that the content is correct and easy to access from multiple devices and platforms. This will lead to proper usability and navigability. We design and develop websites to have excellent performance and ensure accessibility by following the guidelines and standards of WAI (Web Accessibility Initiative). Also, your pages will be validated by W3C. The constant emergence of new devices to access the web makes this an even more vital part for web development.
