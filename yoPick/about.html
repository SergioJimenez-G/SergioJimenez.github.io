Who We Are

After many years being involved in Web and Graphic development, I decided to start a company called yopick to offer great Web and Graphic services to the public.

Sergio Jimenez

Founder and Developer

Our method of work is based on collaboration with the client, to create a relationship of trust, to investigate and meet your needs, and to solve each of your problems in the most clear, simple, and honest way.

We have more than twelve years dedicated to the design and programming world with experience in HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, MYSQL, ACTIONSCRIPT, PHP, PYTHON, and many more.

Our jobs are always supported by the latest trends: HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, etc., taking into account compatibility and usability. We are trained in the best techniques for positioning a website which will expose your product globally.

We are a creative team that has clear objectives and offers responsible and quality service. We will meet delivery dates, consider you as our professional partners, create original designs, and adhere to budgets.

Take a look at some of our latest projects under the portfolio tab. If you like our work, contact us and let's get started creating the best possible project to meet your needs.
